Task Instructions:

Count how many recognizable people are in each image below. To do this, select every recognizable person by clicking on their head. A small red dot will appear wherever you click, and the automatic counter will display how many people you have selected. To delete a red dot, click it again and the automatic counter will remove it.

A "recognizable person" meets ALL of the following criteria:

  • A human being, NOT an animal or an object
  • At least half of their head is in the image (can be front OR back of head)
  • Only counted ONCE (e.g., do NOT double count a person and their mirror reflection)

BONUS OPPORTUNITY:

If your count for every image matches the majority of other Turkers' counts for it (meaning it is most likely accurate), you will receive a performance bonus of $1.00 for this HIT!
